Standalone SVG pseudo-3D globe — orthographic projection with auto-rotation, drag interaction, lat/lng markers, and great-circle arcs. Part of the data-display family in VLLNT UI, it ships as a
machine-readable registry entry — copy the source directly into your app with
the shadcn CLI and own it, no runtime dependency on a component library.
