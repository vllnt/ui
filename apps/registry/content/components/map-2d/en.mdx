Lightweight 2D map primitive — SVG canvas with equirectangular projection, markers, popups, GeoJSON polygon layers, zoom controls, and an optional backdrop image. Part of the data-display family in VLLNT UI, it ships as a
machine-readable registry entry — copy the source directly into your app with
the shadcn CLI and own it, no runtime dependency on a component library.
