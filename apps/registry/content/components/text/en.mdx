Body-text primitive with token-driven sans family and a size/tone/weight scale; polymorphic via `as`. Part of the core family in VLLNT UI, it ships as a
machine-readable registry entry — copy the source directly into your app with
the shadcn CLI and own it, no runtime dependency on a component library.
